How much does it cost?

We have three-tier system for stylists, and you can choose your stylist from any tiers.
Top tier stylists cost generally $80 per package, and low tier stylists cost $30 per package.

Who are the stylists?

They are diverse. Some are fashion industry professionals, fashion enthusiasts, or just someone who has a knack for styling. You can view their profile, reviews, and portfolios.

How does it work?

Let your stylist know about your current needs, and they will assemble a package and send it to you. Return items freely and choose what you keep.
